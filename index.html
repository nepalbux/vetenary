Welcome to Subedi Veterinary Pokhara

Established in 2015, Subedi Veterinary Pokhara has been providing exceptional care for pets in the Pokhara region. Our clinic combines modern veterinary practices with compassionate care to ensure your pets receive the best treatment possible.

Our team of experienced veterinarians and staff are dedicated to the health and wellbeing of your pets. We understand that your pets are family members, and we treat them with the same care and attention we would give to our own.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional veterinary care with compassion and respect for all animals, from pets to livestock.

Our Vision

To be the leading veterinary clinic in Pokhara, known for excellence in both companion animal and livestock care.

Our Comprehensive Services

We offer a wide range of veterinary services to keep your pets and livestock healthy. Our team is equipped with modern facilities to provide the best care possible for all animals.

Routine Check-ups

Regular wellness exams to monitor your pet's health and detect any issues early. Includes physical examination, weight check, and basic health assessment.

Learn more

Vaccinations

Essential vaccinations to protect your pets from common diseases. We follow international protocols and customize vaccination schedules for each pet.

Learn more

Surgery

From routine procedures like spaying and neutering to complex surgeries. Our surgical suite is equipped with modern monitoring equipment.

Learn more

Livestock Care

Specialized care for cattle, buffalo, goats, and other farm animals. We offer on-site visits, vaccinations, reproductive health services, and nutritional guidance.

Learn more

Laboratory Services

In-house diagnostic testing including blood work, urinalysis, fecal exams, and more for quick and accurate diagnosis of your pet's condition.

Learn more

Grooming

Professional grooming services including bathing, haircuts, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and more to keep your pet looking and feeling their best.

Learn more

Emergency Care

Urgent care for pets and livestock in distress. Our clinic offers emergency services during business hours and has partnerships with 24/7 emergency facilities.

Learn more

Mobile Veterinary Services

We bring veterinary care to your farm or home. Ideal for livestock, multiple pets, or animals that experience stress during travel to the clinic.
